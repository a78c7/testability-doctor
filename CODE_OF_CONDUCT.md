# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, practical, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Respect privacy and do not request private credentials or account data.
- Keep issue reports scoped to reproducible repository shapes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Sharing secrets or private account data.
- Encouraging unsafe automation around credentials, payments, or private infrastructure.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate this code of conduct.
